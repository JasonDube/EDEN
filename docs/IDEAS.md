# Game Ideas & Future Features

## Heavy Object Movement
- Engine crane / cherry picker — A-frame from pipes with chain hoist at top
- Rollers — lay pipes on ground, roll heavy objects across them
- Sledge/skid — flat platform (H-slab with handles) you drag things on
- Lever and fulcrum — timber beam + rock to pry things up
- Rope and pulley — hang from ceiling beam, pull to lift (wire system becomes rope)
- Wheelbarrow/cart — model with wheels, load stuff, push around
- Ramp — angled H-slab, slide things up or down

## Weight System
- Metadata: weight in lbs per model
- Heavy (>50 lbs): can't go in hotbar, need crane/cart/rollers
- Medium (10-50 lbs): hotbar but slows player
- Light (<10 lbs): no penalty
- Running weight calculation affects stamina/speed

## Survival Mechanics
- Rain/exposure: movement speed -15%, stamina regen slows, tools take longer
- Cold: health ticks down slowly, crosshair wobbles, food consumption increases
- Heat: water consumption doubles, stamina drains faster
- Shelter detection: indoor flag system already works for this

## Powered Devices (future)
- Water pump — ties pipe + electrical systems together
- Radio/comms tower — contact traders, receive quests
- Heater/furnace — warmth for cold environments
- Fan/air circulator — cool areas, ventilate
- Alarm/siren — security system
- Charging station — for portable tools/flashlights
- Computer — in-game terminal for info/crafting recipes

## World Building
- Post-apocalyptic Earth, AI has taken over
- Robots fighting their own war, humans are rare survivors
- Robot battlefields = source of salvageable parts
- Multi-planet expansion later (different survival pressures per planet)
- Each planet has unique resources, hazards, and building challenges
